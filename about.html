My Father


My father is also incredible and lucky for you, he works in the private sector! I'm pretty sure that every single moment of his life has been unusual in some fashion and there is no way I would be able to accurately recount it all. If I am successful in convincing him to write a memoir I'll be able to link that in here later. Random fun fact, he used to hold the world record for fastest Rubik's cube solving.
He has also lived in NYC his whole life and attended Stuyvesant High School, then going on to Dartmouth College. When he was in high school, he was part of the chess team and helped them to win their second national-championship trophy. If you want to read more about that, here is the link to the NY Times article that discusses it: https://nytimes.com/chesschamps/. I would describe him as a fun-loving, kind hearted genius.
