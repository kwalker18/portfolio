Music

I really love music, both playing and listening to it. I have been playing classical violin for roughly 12 years and don't plan to stop anytime soon. In 6th grade I started playing alto sax as well and I'm now in my school's senior jazz band. Recreationally I like to mess around on the guitar and piano but I'm not very good at all. I play the latter two instruments mainly to recreate the songs of some of my favorite artists. At the moment I listen to a lot of indie/bedroom pop music and my favorite artists within that genre are Still Woozy, beabadoobee, Yellow Days, Summer Salt, Ralph Castelli and Ritt Momney. There are so many others that I love but that seems like a solid start. One of my absolute favorite bands used to be Bane's World but unfortunately I can no longer listen to their music on any platforms since I don't support sexual assault.
